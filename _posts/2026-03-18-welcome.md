Welcome to our new blog! This is where we'll share in-depth analysis, workers' stories, and updates from our campaigns.

## Why a Blog?

While our [Substack commentary](https://workersmakepossible.substack.com/) covers major pieces, this blog lets us share quicker updates, event recaps, and resources for workers.

## What to Expect

- **Campaign updates** — progress on our key demands
- **Worker stories** — first-hand accounts from workers
- **Resources** — guides and tools to support workers facing issues
- **Event coverage** — recaps from actions and events

Stay tuned, and thank you for your solidarity. ✊
