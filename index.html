Workers Make Possible
Workers Make Possible is committed to advancing workers' rights in Singapore.
We deepen public understanding of labour issues by offering critical labour analysis, rallying workers around key demands, and supporting workers by providing direct assistance when they face crises. Since workers' perspectives are not often covered by the mainstream media, our work is also focused on telling these important stories, and mobilising wider public support.
